Judo team Beersheba Israel, identity

Judo team Beersheba Israel is a regional judo team and the city judo club.

The visual system is based on regional and national symbols. It is designed to be bold, but not aggressive or obtrusive. It is important to unite the team while still keeping some space for unique characteristics of the players.
