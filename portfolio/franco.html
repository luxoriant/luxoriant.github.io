Franco, identity

Franco is a construction company with several divisions. They produce concrete, build houses and offices, transport materials and construct roads.

The customers are tired of old-school construction companies with confusing processes, unexpected payments and slow pace. That is why Franco offers clever logistics with transparent cash flow and modern services.

The identity is about being open, clear and friendly. It reflects in colors, typography and other graphics. Visuals stay serious and familiar. Yet, now they have their own, engaging personality.
