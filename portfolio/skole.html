Skole, identity

Skole is a town aimed at developing its touristic potential.

Primary target groups are "wild", cultural and sport tourists. A strong visual identity will make them more eager to recommend this place to their friends.

Additional target groups are citizens, foreign investors, potential tourists interested in city attractions and family leisure.

The identity system considers different scenarios of implementation. Typographic layouts and photos for the government. English logo and European style for the foreign investors. Realistic illustrations for the actual tourists. Conceptual graphics for potential tourists.
