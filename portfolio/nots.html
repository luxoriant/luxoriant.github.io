NOTS, brand identity

Norsk Organisasjon For Terrengsykling is a unity of mountain bikers. They aim to popularize the biking culture, to safeguard cyclists' rights to free use of forest and land in Norway.

The visual identity uses unobtrusive graphics and typography as it needs to have a formal face to represent bicyclists in their disputes with authorities.

The dynamic layouts are made to empower participants of the organization. Motivational posters use the outlined logo to convey transparency and accessibility the brand strives for.
