Visual impairment, identity

Visual impairment is a decreased ability to see.

The identity features the blind smile, the braille dot and the optical lens. The symbol unites people with visual impairment and gives others more ways to communicate efficiently with the former. The graphics allow you to identify visual impairment friendly places, tools, events.
