Judo team B’’rsheva Israel, identity

Judo team B’’rsheva Israel is a judo club and a regional judo team. The adult and junior teams compete in local and national tournaments.

JTBSI is about being proud of the origins, staying authentic and unite. The team members are energetic and self-motivated.

The identity features national and local symbols, the Star of David, tamarix tree flower, colors of state and city flags.
