Ukrainian Flag Company, identity

Ukrainian Flag Company is a flag producer. It also supports pro-Ukrainian social initiatives and war veterans.

The main goal of the business is to produce qualitative flags under the uniform brand which is the guarantee of the quality of products and services.

The target groups are progressive youth, pro-Ukrainian citizens, business representatives, sportsmen, fans, people in military.

The firm style is one of the key marketing advantages of the business, product protection method, the guarantee of the quality. The goal of graphics is to establish a direct connection “flags — Ukrainian Flag Company”.
