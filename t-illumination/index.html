T*Illumination. Bringing community to light

T*Illumination is a non-profitable project oriented at informing and mobilizing society around the rights of trans*people.

The metaphor of the identity is to highlight the information about trans*life, to be a beacon for community members.

My job was also to create a simple generator for text-only Instagram posts, for operative announcements. You can try it out right here, right now.
