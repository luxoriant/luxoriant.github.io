Polarsol Village, identity

Polarsol Village is an energy efficient settlement.

The brand audience consists of modern, progressive family people aimed at living outside the city. They know something about technology and care enough to observe most of the available opportunities on the market before moving in.

Those are happy people. They value relatives and home comfort more than any technologies, percentages and marks. That's why the brand visual system is warm and humane.
