Mitipi

Mitipi is a Swiss insurance company. It combines smart home technology and modern insurance for a safe and convenient life.

There are two key products. The first one, Kevin, is a smart home assistant. The second one, Maria, is a customizable insurance package.

Details

The company is modern and innovative. It is built by tech guys who use the product in their homes, and love to add more and more useful features to it.

This pioneering company does not have direct competitors. However, the existing challenge is to promote the new market field to the average customer of 50 and older ages.

It is important for the brand and product to look familiar, understandable and reliable. The traditional Swiss style is perfect for that matter.

Not to fall into the field of vintage “Swiss 50’s” vibe, the brand uses dynamic layouts and advanced graphic techniques. Although the core of the identity has a pure Swiss vibe, the marketing pieces use shades, noise, gradients, overlays and textures.
