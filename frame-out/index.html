Frame Out, logo concept 1

The concept is bold and fresh. It is brave, which better suits non-profit organization. It symbolizes a circle for dialogue, inclusion, empowerment; a constant wheel of change, of education and growth.

In smaller versions, it also creates a white star in the center, because every person has unique, boundless potential.


The concept utilizes Montserrat font for headings and logo, as it is a bold choice. Yet, for more compact text blocks and other headings in other languages, it is better to use Noto Sans.
