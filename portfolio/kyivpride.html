KyivPride, identity concept

KyivPride is the biggest LGBT+ pride in Ukraine. The participants aim to protect human rights, and the ability of LGBT+ community to use them.

The identity puts the motive of the force of change into the vivid visual form. It is various and unique by design. Thus, it easily embraces people and events.
