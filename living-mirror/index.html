Living Mirror, identity

Living Mirror is a “playback” theater, an improvisational theater based on the stories of the audience. It brings our own lives to focus, helps to connect on the basis of shared experience.

According to the concept, playback is both a mirror for our stories and a portal to the world of feelings. Minimalistic, clean layouts help to lower the cost of maintaining the identity.
