Franco, identity

Franco Group is a construction company with several divisions. It produces concrete, builds houses and offices, transports materials and constructs roads.

The target markets are both end customers and construction companies. They get to choose among many providers of good products. So the real difference is the logistics of the processes and the reliable infrastructure the company has.

The company is new, fresh and contemporary by means of management and services which is a key marketing feature. Reliability, accessibility and transparency are the key parts of the brand personality.

It was important not to get too fancy; to stay fresh and full of space and air. The brand architecture is complex, but the surface of it stays modern and simple.
